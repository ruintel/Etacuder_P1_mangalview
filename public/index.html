About Us

We provide a comfortable and homely atmosphere for all our guests. Located on the main road near Gulab Bagh, our hotel is an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers.
Our core values are simplicity, quality, and service. We provide delicious and healthy pure vegetarian dining options that align with our satvic philosophy. Our commitment to creating a welcoming environment for families is reflected in our decision to not serve alcohol.
Choose from our range of rooms, from standard to suites, all designed to provide a comfortable and relaxing stay. Our dedication to service has earned us positive feedback from guests around the world, and we aim to exceed your expectations.
Thank you for choosing us for your stay. We look forward to providing you with an unforgettable experience.

Rooftop Restaurant

Located atop the hotel, the restaurant boasts stunning views of the City Palace, one of Udaipur's most iconic landmarks. As you enjoy your meal, you can take in the stunning views of the palace and the surrounding city, which offer a picturesque glimpse into India's rich history and vibrant culture. The restaurant serves a variety of vegetarian dishes that are both delicious and healthy, making it a great choice for anyone looking for a culinary adventure in Udaipur.

Inhouse Dinning

Our in-house dining also provides a convenient option for guests, eliminating the need to venture out for every meal and allowing them to fully relax and enjoy their stay. For guests seeking a peaceful dining experience, we offer buffet breakfast and vegetarian meals is an excellent choice from the best chefs in the town, and beyond.
